From the Garden State Parkway:
  • Take the Garden State Parkway to Exit 145 (Route 280 West).
  • Take Route 280 West to Route 80 West, to Route 15 North and follow directions below from Route 15.
 
From Route 287:
  • Take Route 287 North to Route 80 West, to Route 15 North and follow directions below from Route 15.
 
From Route 206:
  • Take Route 206 North to Route 80 West, to Route 15 North and follow directions below from Route 15.
 
From Route 15 North:
  • Take Route 15 North for 13 miles.
  • Make a right onto White Lake Road.
  • Camp Sacajawea is on the right hand side, approximately 1 mile from Route 15.
* If you come to a T-intersection at the end of White Lake Road, you have passed the camp.
