Are you a Hero?

For one weekend a month, throw caution to the wind
and blaze a heroʼs trail in the capital city of Avalon –
a place where all manner of men and mystical beings congregate
in search of adventure, glory, and fun.
Whether honing your magical skills at The Guild of Arcane Lore,
laboring in The Armory to craft extraordinary weapons,
trading all kinds of wares at the Gold & Glory merchantʼs outpost,
or exploring the far expanses of the world of Midlantia,
your character will be an integral part of our living story.

LAIRE is open to players of all backgrounds age 14 and up.
Your first event is free, complete with new player training
and an open community happy to welcome new faces to the game.
