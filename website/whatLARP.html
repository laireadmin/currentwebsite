What is a LARP

A live action role playing (LARP) game is a collective fantasy world similar to the role playing game Dungeons & Dragons™ –â€“ but in the flesh. Instead of a "Dungeon Master" verbally narrating the story, a Plot Committee writes and guides overarching story lines in real time and real locations. The players of a live action role playing game create their own story minute by minute, month by month, perhaps even year by year. In our LARP, players physically represent their characters for one whole weekend a month - performing all of the actions that the character performs. There are no dice rolls to determine if you can move quietly, no "rock, paper, scissors" to determine the outcome of a battle, and no chits to move about on a board to show positions. You the player get to sneak around, fight the battles and be aware of your surroundings in real time.
